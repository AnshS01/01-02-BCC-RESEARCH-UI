Senior Business Analyst-Semiconductor

BCC Research provides a comprehensive analysis of global market sizing, forecasting and industry intelligence, covering markets where advances in science and technology improve the quality, standard and sustainability of businesses, economies and lives.

We are seeking a highly motivated Business Analyst to join our team and contribute to our in-depth market research efforts in the semiconductor industry. As a Business Analyst, you will play a crucial role in collecting, analysing, and presenting market data and insights related to semiconductors.

BCC Head offices are based in Boston, USA, however, to ensure we are able to recruit the best talent, we are opening this role up globally – no matter where you are based, we look forward to receiving your CV/application.

Roles and Responsibilities
  • Conduct comprehensive market research on the semiconductor industry, including market size, trends, competitive landscape, and emerging technologies
  • Collect and analyse data from various sources, such as industry reports, market studies, and financial statements, to identify key market opportunities and challenges.
  • Develop and maintain databases of industry statistics, market forecasts, and competitor profiles
  • Collaborate with cross-functional teams to gather requirements and deliver market research reports and insights tailored to specific business needs
  • Present findings and recommendations to internal stakeholders, including executives, product managers, and marketing teams
  • Stay up-to-date with the latest advancements and innovations in the semiconductor industry through continuous monitoring of industry publications, research papers, and industry events
  • Minimum 7 years' experience in relevant industry sector or business insights related to
    Semiconductor Material | Testing, Inspection, and Certification | Display | Lighting Semiconductor Components (Sensor | Processor | Memory | Batteries| Electronic Systems
  • Ability to work independently as well as collaboratively within a team environment
  • Comprehensive knowledge of market research principles and practices with demonstrated capabilities in both qualitative and quantitative research
  • Strong analytical capabilities, ability to interpret and draw industry-relevant insight from multiple primary and secondary research sources
  • Exceptional verbal and written communication skills with the capability to interact across all levels (internal & external) and support decision-making
  • Excellent multitasking capabilities and the ability to work effectively in an environment of tight deadlines and constant change
  • Strong attention to detail with an emphasis on accuracy, consistency, and quality
  • Bachelor's degree in Engineering, Materials or a related field. Advanced degree preferred
